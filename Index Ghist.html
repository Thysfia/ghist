What Is Ghist?

Ghist is an educational website made for highschool students who are either learning geology or just really REALLY into geology. Providing a lot of geo-based informations from reliable sources around the world, Ghist aims for students to easily find the learning materials they need, saving them the time they would normally spend searching for information on the internet.

What Can You Find in Ghist?

Minerals

Definition of minerals, their types, and their characteristics.

Rocks

Rock cycle, types of rocks, and rock distribution.

Landforms

Geomorphology, stratigraphy, and landform distribution.

Maps

Interactive geologic map featured with rock and volcano distribution.

Collections

Archive of photos, videos, and illustrations about geology.
